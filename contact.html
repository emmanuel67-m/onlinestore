Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping rates?

Our shipping rates vary depending on your location and the items you're purchasing. You can view the exact shipping cost at checkout before completing your order.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in the same condition that you received them. Please check our Returns page for more details.
